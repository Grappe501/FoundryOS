# Civic Engagement — Permanent Domain Brief

> Domain slug: `civic-engagement` · Vertical: Citizen Engagement / Civics

---

## Outcome

**Become an Informed Citizen**

Not: consume election news.

---

## Care Reason

Civic participation compounds — informed citizens build stronger communities, lead boards, serve as poll workers, and mentor the next generation of engaged neighbors.

---

## Paths

```txt
Road to Civic Engagement
Road to Informed Voter
Road to Community Advocate
Road to Local Leader
```

---

## Mastery Levels

```txt
Curious Citizen → Engaged Citizen → Community Advocate → Civic Leader → Steward → Mentor
```

---

## Projects

- Find Polling Place
- Research Local Offices
- Attend a Candidate Forum
- Learn Local Government Structure
- Volunteer as Poll Worker
- Serve on a Board or Commission

---

## Community

**Local Civic Circle** — shared projects, forums, poll worker coordination, board service mentorship.
