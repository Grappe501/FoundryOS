## Permanent Domain: Civic Engagement

**Outcome:** Become an Informed Citizen

**Not:** I want election news.

**Transformation:**

```txt
I want to be a more informed and engaged citizen.
```

```txt
Where do I vote?
What's on my ballot?
Who is running?
What does this office do?
What district am I in?
```

Municipal · School Board · Primary · Runoff · Midterm · Presidential · Special Elections
