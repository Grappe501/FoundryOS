# Foundry Student

> One of the biggest opportunities — students spend years inside life leverage domains.

---

## Product Concept

**Foundry Student** — the entry point for Grades 6–12 into transformation domains parents and schools will pay for.

Not a homework app. Not a course catalog. A **becoming** platform.

---

## Segments

| Segment | Grades | Entry Question |
|---------|--------|----------------|
| Middle School | 6–8 | *What kind of adult do I want to become?* |
| High School | 9–12 | *What skills will change my life?* |

---

## Priority Outcomes (Student Channel)

Students don't pick domains — they pick **outcomes**:

```txt
Financially Literate Adult
AI Builder
Public Speaker
Community Leader
Entrepreneur
Researcher
Programmer
```

Each outcome maps to a Life Leverage Domain with age-appropriate paths and projects.

---

## Example: Financial Independence for Students

**Middle School path:** Road to Financially Literate Teen

Projects:

- Open first savings account (with parent)
- Track spending for 30 days
- Build first budget

**High School path:** Road to Investor → Wealth Builder

Projects:

- Buy first stock
- Analyze first company
- Build retirement projection
- Create emergency fund

Evidence feeds reputation and mastery — visible progress parents can see.

## Relationship to Foundry Identity

One account · one graph · student paths become adult mastery titles.

A middle schooler who completes "Budget Completed" evidence at 13 carries that into their Foundry Identity at 25.

**Foundry remembers.**
