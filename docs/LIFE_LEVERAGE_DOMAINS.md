# Life Leverage Domains

> Domains that dramatically change life outcomes — not hobbies.

---

## Tier Model

Foundry organizes domains in two layers:

```txt
Life Leverage Domains     ← parents pay · schools recommend · students spend years inside
Identity Domains          ← lifelong pursuits across categories
```

### Identity Domain Categories

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Academic | Mathematics, Computer Science, History |
| Skills | Public Speaking, Negotiation, Leadership |
| Hobbies | Poker, Gardening, Photography |
| Careers | Campaign Management, Engineering |
| Lifestyles | Homesteading, Fitness, Parenting |
| Communities | Bourbon, Books, Movies |

### Life Leverage Domains (Tier Above)

Domains where transformation compounds across decades:

| Domain | Outcome | Why It Matters |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| **Financial Independence** | Achieve financial freedom | Parents pay · schools struggle · students need it |
| **AI Builder** | Become an AI Builder | Career leverage · every industry reshaping |
| **Public Speaking** | Confident communicator | Career + civic + leadership multiplier |
| **Civic Engagement** | Informed citizen → community leader | Schools need · recurring election cycles |

Hobbies (BBQ, Movies, Poker) build identity and community. **Life leverage domains change trajectory.**
