# Foundry Compass

## Evolution

| Today | Future |
|-------|--------|
| What should I do next? | What direction should I be moving? |

The next step is not always a project. Sometimes it's:

- find a mentor
- join a community
- practice a skill
- reflect on a failure
- help someone else

The graph should understand those distinctions.
