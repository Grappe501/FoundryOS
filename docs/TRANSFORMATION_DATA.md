# Transformation Data

Foundry's most valuable long-term asset — not content, not AI, not courses, not the knowledge graph alone.

## What It Is

Aggregated, anonymized patterns of what actually predicts success on paths:

```txt
10,000 people followed the Road to Public Speaker.
The projects that most predicted success were:
1. Toastmasters-style practice
2. Community presentations
3. Teaching others
Average time to mastery: 2.4 years
```

## What It Is Not

- Personal data
- Generated content
- AI answers

## Why It Matters

Future users navigate more effectively when the platform learns which projects, milestones, and communities correlate with completion and mastery.
