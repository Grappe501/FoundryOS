# Foundry Level Model

> **Courses get people in. Identity keeps them for years.**

## The Reframe (locked)

Old framing:

```txt
Level 1 = Learn
Level 2 = Live
Level 3 = Lead
```

**Correct framing — five layers:**

```txt
Layer 1  Explore     — discover the world
Layer 2  Practice     — do the work (missions, investigations, challenges)
Layer 3  Identity     — who am I here? (artifacts, passport, memory)
Layer 4  Influence    — who trusts my judgment?
Layer 5  Legacy       — permanent record over years
```

Also expressed as:

```txt
Explore → Practice → Identity → Influence → Legacy
```

Subtle on paper. Changes everything in product design.

| Layer | Question | Examples |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Explore** | What is this world? | Atlas, lore, lessons, tools, rabbit holes |
| **Practice** | What do I actually do? | Missions, detective cases, challenges, experiences |
| **Identity** | Who am I here? | Collections, artifacts, passport, journal, continuity |
| **Influence** | Who trusts me? | Reviews, recommendations, hosting, groups |
| **Legacy** | What did I build? | Timeline, decade archive, artifact history |

See `docs/WORLD_ECOSYSTEM_MODEL.md` for the full ecosystem model.

## Psychological Shift

Today:

```txt
Come learn something.
```

Target:

```txt
Come belong somewhere.
```

People spend years on Goodreads, Untappd, Reddit — not for content. For:

```txt
MY profile · MY shelf · MY reviews · MY discoveries
MY reputation · MY friends · MY recommendations
```

**Ownership · Status · Recognition · Contribution** — where obsession comes from.

## What Exists vs What's Missing

```txt
Built (Explore + Practice + Identity signals):
  Atlas · Lore · Missions · Detective · Collections
  Consequences · Events · Narrative · Continuity

Missing (Identity + Influence infrastructure):
  Artifact Engine · Personal DB · Review/Recommend
  Group · Host · Passport shell · Reputation · Legacy
```

The reactive loop (034J→P) closes **personalized memory**. The **040 block** closes **identity infrastructure**.
