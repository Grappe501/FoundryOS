# The Foundry Equation

Simplify the entire company to five phases. Everything else is implementation.

```txt
Potential → Capability → Agency → Contribution → Legacy
```

## Phases

| Phase | Question | Implements |
|-------|----------|------------|
| **Potential** | What do I want to become? | Outcomes, Life Journeys, Purpose |
| **Capability** | What knowledge, skills, habits, and experience do I need? | Knowledge, Mastery, Academy, Paths, Domains |
| **Agency** | Can I take meaningful action? | Projects, Next Best Step, Momentum, Transformation Impact |
| **Contribution** | How do I use those capabilities in the real world? | Community, Roles, Identity, Evidence |
| **Legacy** | How do I help others and leave something behind? | Mentorship, Legacy, Transformation Data |

## Agency — The Bridge

> Agency is the bridge between knowing and doing. Without it, people consume. With it, people transform.

Foundry optimizes for **agency** — not knowledge, mastery, or completion.

## Mission

> Foundry helps people become who they are capable of becoming.

This works for bourbon enthusiasts, gardeners, programmers, campaign managers, students, professionals, retirees — everyone.
