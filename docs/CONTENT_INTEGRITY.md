# Content Integrity — No Fabricated People or Marketing

> **Create spaces. Do not invent profiles.**

## Hard Rules

1. **No fabricated biographies** — do not publish detailed people profiles unless sourced as `editorial` or `verified` and tied to evidence.
2. **No marketing-as-fact** — catalog metadata (bottle name, proof, producer link) is fine; invented narratives about real people are not.
3. **Slots, not stories** — leaders, reviewers, mentors, and hosts are **empty slots** until community or editorial content fills them.
4. **Graph references ≠ profiles** — a graph edge to a person name is a **connection intent**, not permission to generate a bio page.
5. **Operator counts truth** — 034U reports **referenced entities** vs **verified profiles** separately.

## Content Sources (locked)

```txt
generated   — factory draft, publish-gated
community   — user-submitted, evidence-backed
editorial   — Foundry-verified
verified    — primary-source confirmed
```

## What To Build Instead

| Instead of | Build |
|------------|-------|
| Fake master distiller page | Leader slot linked to producer + review space |
| Invented review quotes | Review engine slot (040E) — empty until real |
| AI biography | Atlas term + graph connections + why_should_i_care |
| Marketing copy as lore | Structured atlas entry with source discipline |
